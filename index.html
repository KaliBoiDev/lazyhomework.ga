LazyHomework

What is LazyHomework?

LazyHomework is a service for Carmel Students to have their homework done for them. This is primarily targeted toward 8th grade students, but the service is open to 6-8th graders. This service applies all classes(excluding electives/rotations), although some more than others. This service may be limited in the LA and History classes, however I am still willing to help out with work from these classes.

Liability

If you decide to work the me, I take no liability for any poor grades received on work that I completed. By working with me, you agree to taking any and all responsibility associated with turning in the work. While this is true, I will always provide the best work and the most effort that I can for my clients.

Pricing

As there are too many varied assignments to assign a price to all of them, pricing will fluxuate depending on the specific details of the assignment given.
